Privacy by design

Remindox stores everything locally on your device. We collect no personal data, require no account, and make no network requests. Your data is yours — always.

1. About Remindox

Remindox is a personal organiser app that helps you track product warranties, recurring bills and subscriptions, and life admin tasks (such as dental check-ups, car servicing, or passport renewals). The app sends you local reminders before important dates so nothing slips through the cracks.

2. Information You Enter

You voluntarily enter the following types of information into the app. This data is stored only on your device and is never sent anywhere.

Warranties — Product name, store name, purchase date, purchase price, currency, warranty duration, expiry date, product category, claim status, notes, and an optional product photo.
Bills & Subscriptions — Service name, category, billing amount, currency, billing cycle (monthly, yearly, etc.), start date, and an optional icon.
Life Admin Tasks — Task title, emoji, category, recurrence frequency, last completed date, next due date, preferred reminder time of day, and optional notes.

3. Camera & Photo Library

When you add a product photo or scan a purchase receipt, the app requests access to your camera or photo library. This permission is used only for the feature you triggered — it is never used in the background.

Receipt scanning uses on-device text recognition (Google ML Kit) to try to extract the product name, purchase date, and total price from the image. This processing happens entirely on your device. The image and any extracted text are never uploaded or shared.

4. Local Notifications

Remindox schedules local push notifications to remind you about:

Warranty expiry — multiple reminders (e.g. 30, 7, and 1 day before expiry).
Subscription renewal — a reminder before the next billing date.
Life admin tasks — reminders before the next due date, at the time of day you choose.

These notifications are generated and delivered entirely by your device's operating system. No external notification server is involved. You can disable notifications at any time in your device's system settings.

5. No Internet Connection Required

Remindox is a fully offline app. It makes no network requests. Your data never leaves your device over the internet.

6. No Account, No Sign-In

You do not need to create an account, provide an email address, or sign in with any service to use Remindox. We do not collect any personal identifiers.

7. No Analytics or Tracking

Remindox contains no analytics SDKs, crash reporting tools, advertising networks, or any other third-party code that observes how you use the app. We have zero visibility into your usage.

8. Data Storage & Security

All your data is stored in a local database (Isar) on your device. It is subject to the same protections your operating system provides to app data — other apps cannot access it. We do not have access to your device or your data.

9. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your data with any third party — ever. Because everything stays on your device, there is nothing for us to share.

10. Backup & Data Export

Remindox includes an optional backup feature (Settings → Backup & Restore) that lets you export all your data as a JSON file and save or share it yourself. This export is:

Initiated entirely by you — nothing is exported automatically.
Saved to your own device or shared to a destination you choose (such as a cloud drive, email, or messaging app). Remindox does not send the file anywhere on its own.
A plain JSON text file containing your warranties, subscriptions, and life admin tasks. Photo file paths are not included — only text data.
Restorable at any time from the same Settings screen. Restored data is merged with what is already on your device.

We do not receive, store, or have access to any backup file you create. The backup goes wherever you send it — to no one else.

11. Your Control Over Your Data

You are in full control of your data at all times:

Delete any warranty, subscription, or task individually from within the app.
Cancel scheduled notifications by deleting the related item.
Export a full backup of your data at any time via Settings → Backup & Restore.
Uninstalling the app permanently erases all locally stored data from your device.

12. Children's Privacy

Remindox is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children. Since no information is collected from any user, this applies by default.

13. Changes to This Policy

If we make material changes to this privacy policy, the updated version will be included in a new app update on the app store. We encourage you to review this policy whenever the app is updated. Continued use of the app after an update constitutes your acceptance of the revised policy.
